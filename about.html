About Ms. Lord

Ms. Kimberly Lord has a wealth of experience in university admission, as well as college counseling in independent school settings. She has worked in admission and financial aid at universities as varied as Rice University, Harvard, and the University of North Texas. She has also directed college counseling programs at a variety of independent schools, including Saint Stephen's Episcopal School, Brookwood School, and Antwerp International School.

Ms. Lord has visited over 130 university campuses in 21 states and five countries, and is well-versed with schools throughout the United States and internationally. She holds a bachelor’s degree from Vassar College and a master’s degree from Harvard University.

In her counseling, Ms. Lord strives to help students build 21st century skills and think about future careers, which may or may not exist yet, as they work through their college choice. She focuses on the big-picture questions about what a student wants out of life, and which schools will best fit the student's needs.
